Workflow Guide

  1. Start with Dashboard to see overview.
  2. Go to Conversations → click a conversation to see messages, intent, score, qualification.
  3. Go to Customers → click a customer for profile, CRM history, timeline, past temperatures.
  4. Check Support Cases and Escalations queues.
  5. View Recommendations panel.
  6. Inspect Knowledge → open a document for metadata, chunk previews.
  7. Browse Audit Log for all system actions.
  8. Use Corrections to submit human corrections or mark responses.
  9. Use the Tools quick links to test engines and APIs.
